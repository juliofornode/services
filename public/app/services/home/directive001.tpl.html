Other important notes:

  • Services are designed to be injected in the other components of your application. Therefore, their creation is very much a part of Angular's dependency injection system. Objects that know how to create injectable services are called providers. In order to create a service, we must first create the provider that will build it with the built-in service $provider.
  • The basic process to create a service: $provide creates the provider, and the provider creates the service.
